The Cow

A Widely Domesticated Animal


Cow is the most widely domesticated animal all over the world for milk. Someone who is part of the Indian society realizes the significance of cow. It is very common in Indian households to find milk and milk products. Cow is a highly useful animal for humans. Cows, through milk and other by-products, sustain billions of families throughout the world.
